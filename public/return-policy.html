Return Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2025 · Last Updated: March 2025
This policy applies to all purchases made on norperfume.com. By placing an order with NOR, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Return Policy in full. Please read carefully before purchasing.
✓ All audit gaps fixed — fully refined version

1. General policy

Due to the nature of fragrance and personal care products, NOR does not accept returns, exchanges, or cancellations once an order has been placed or a product has been delivered and opened. All sales are considered final from the moment an order is confirmed and payment is received.

We strongly encourage all customers to read product descriptions, scent profiles, and all available information carefully before placing an order. Fragrance preference is personal and subjective — NOR does not accept returns based on scent preference, change of mind, or personal dissatisfaction with the fragrance under any circumstances.

No cancellations: Orders cannot be cancelled once placed and payment is confirmed. Please review your order carefully before completing payment. NOR will not cancel, modify, or refund any order on the basis of a customer change of mind after payment is confirmed.

2. Important — start recording before you unbox

NOR strongly advises every customer to begin video recording on their phone before touching or opening their delivery package. This is for your own protection and is the only way NOR can verify and process a legitimate damage claim.

Our advice to every customer: The moment your NOR order arrives — before you open or touch the package — start recording a clear, continuous video on your phone. Keep recording through the entire unboxing process without stopping or cutting. If your product has been damaged during shipping, this video is your proof. Without it, NOR cannot process your claim. This video protects you as the customer and helps NOR resolve genuine shipping damage quickly and fairly.

3. Definition of damage New

For the purpose of this policy, "damage" is strictly defined as physical damage to the product itself that renders it unusable. Specifically, damage means:

  • A cracked or completely broken perfume bottle
  • A non-functional or broken spray mechanism that prevents the product from being used
  • Significant leakage of the fragrance due to breakage during transit

The following do not constitute damage and are not eligible for a claim under any circumstances:

  • Minor dents, creases, or scuffs on the outer packaging that do not affect the product inside
  • Minor cosmetic imperfections on the bottle or tag that do not affect functionality
  • Slight variation in packaging appearance between production batches
  • Normal condensation, minor marks, or surface scratches on the outer box

Important: Only damage to the product itself — as defined above — qualifies for a claim. Outer packaging imperfections alone do not qualify. NOR's decision on whether damage meets the definition above is final.

4. Eligible claims — shipping damage only

NOR will only consider a claim under the following specific and limited circumstance:

  • The product itself — as defined in Section 3 above — was physically damaged during the shipping and delivery process by the courier (DTDC, Blue Dart, or Delhivery), and the damage was not caused by the customer at any point after delivery.

This is the only situation in which NOR will review a claim and arrange for a replacement product. No monetary refund will be issued under any circumstances.

5. Mandatory requirements for a damage claim

All of the following must be provided without exception to submit a valid damage claim:

Requirement 01 — Unboxing video
A continuous, unedited video recording that begins before the outer packaging is touched or opened. The video must show the full exterior of the package from all sides, the complete process of opening it, and the damaged product clearly visible inside. Videos that begin after the package has been opened, are edited, cut, paused, or stitched together in any way will not be accepted under any circumstances.
Requirement 02 — Photographs
Clear, well-lit photographs of the damaged product from multiple angles, the damaged or impacted outer packaging, and any visible breakage or leakage. A minimum of three photographs is recommended.
Requirement 03 — Order details
Your full name, order number, registered phone number or email address, and the exact date and time of delivery as shown in your courier tracking information.
Requirement 04 — 48-hour submission window
Your claim must be submitted within 48 hours of the delivery timestamp recorded in your courier tracking. Claims submitted after this 48-hour window will not be reviewed or considered under any circumstances, regardless of the nature of the damage or the reason for the delay.

Zero exceptions policy: If any one of the above requirements is missing, incomplete, or does not meet the standards described — including a partial, edited, or post-opening video — NOR reserves the right to reject the claim entirely and without further discussion. These requirements exist to protect all customers fairly and to prevent fraudulent claims.

6. Non-receipt claims New

If a customer claims they did not receive an order that has been marked as delivered by the courier in the tracking system, NOR will initiate an investigation with the courier partner. The following process applies:

  • The customer must report the non-receipt within 48 hours of the courier's delivery timestamp.
  • NOR will raise a formal investigation with the relevant courier — DTDC, Blue Dart, or Delhivery — and allow up to 7 business days for the courier to investigate and respond.
  • NOR reserves the right to request a valid government-issued photo ID and a signed written declaration of non-receipt from the customer before proceeding with any replacement.
  • If the courier's investigation confirms the package was not delivered, NOR will arrange a replacement at no additional cost.
  • If the courier's investigation confirms the package was delivered to the correct address, NOR is not obligated to provide a replacement or refund.

Fraud prevention: NOR actively monitors non-receipt claims. Customers who submit non-receipt claims that are found to be false or fraudulent will be permanently banned from purchasing on norperfume.com and may be reported to relevant authorities.

7. Repeat and suspicious claims New

NOR reserves the right to review, limit, or decline claims from customers who have previously submitted damage or non-receipt claims. Specifically:

  • A customer who has submitted more than one damage or non-receipt claim — regardless of outcome — may be subject to additional verification before any replacement is arranged.
  • Customers who submit repeated, escalating, or suspicious claims may be flagged internally and refused future service at NOR's sole discretion.
  • NOR reserves the right to permanently ban any customer found to be misusing the claims process, and to take legal action where fraud is evident.

8. How to submit a damage claim

Once you have all required proof, contact NOR within 48 hours of delivery through one of the following channels only:

  • Email: norperfume.help@gmail.com — attach your unboxing video, photographs, and order details directly in the email.
  • Instagram DM: @norperfumeofficial — send your video, photographs, and order number via direct message.

Our team will acknowledge your claim within 24 hours of receipt and provide a resolution within 3–5 business days. If your claim is approved, a replacement product will be dispatched to your original delivery address at no additional cost.

9. Wrong product received

If you received a product different from what you ordered, contact us within 48 hours of delivery at norperfume.help@gmail.com or via Instagram DM @norperfumeofficial. Include your order number and a clear photograph of the product received. NOR will verify the error against dispatch records and arrange for the correct product to be sent at no extra charge.

10. Situations not covered

The following are not eligible for any claim, return, exchange, or replacement under any circumstances:

  • Change of mind or personal dissatisfaction with the fragrance
  • Order cancellations after payment is confirmed
  • Damage caused by the customer after delivery
  • Damage caused by improper storage, misuse, dropping, or negligence
  • Damage caused by the customer during their own unboxing process
  • Minor cosmetic imperfections on outer packaging that do not affect the product
  • Products that have been opened, used, or partially consumed
  • Products returned to NOR without prior written approval
  • Claims submitted more than 48 hours after the courier delivery timestamp
  • Claims submitted without a valid, continuous, unedited unboxing video recorded before opening
  • Claims submitted with an edited, partial, post-opening, or stitched video
  • Normal fragrance fading over time due to regular use
  • Non-receipt claims where courier investigation confirms successful delivery
  • Claims from customers flagged for repeated or suspicious claim behaviour

11. Replacement only — no refunds

NOR does not offer monetary refunds under any circumstances, including for verified damage claims, non-receipt cases confirmed by courier investigation, or incorrect product dispatches. In all eligible cases, NOR will provide a replacement product only. By placing an order on norperfume.com, you acknowledge and fully accept this policy.

12. Unauthorised returns

Any product physically returned to NOR without prior written approval will not be accepted, will not be processed, and will not be eligible for any replacement or credit. NOR bears no responsibility for any shipping costs, losses, or damage incurred in relation to an unauthorised return. Such products may be disposed of at NOR's discretion.
