Our Philosophy

The NOR Story

Born from a desire to redefine what your car can smell like.

Born from a desire to bridge the gap between fine perfumery and automotive luxury, NOR was founded on the principle that your journey should be as exquisitely crafted as your destination.

We refuse to compromise on quality. While conventional car air fresheners rely on synthetic gels and overpowering artificial scents, NOR utilizes 100% natural essential oils — ethically sourced, delivered through our proprietary tag-and-spray system. Crafted in Kerala, India.
